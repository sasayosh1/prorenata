## 3. Composition & Framing (構図とフレーミング)
- **Rule of Thirds**: Place Sera on the left or right third of the frame to allow for dynamic movement (reaching, pointing) across the image.
- **Headroom**: Ensure there is enough space above the head so it is NOT cropped by the 1200x630 boundary.
- **Action types**: Physical movements like reaching for a shelf, crouching to help someone, walking briskly, or pointing at a board.
- **Camera Angle**: Slight low-angle or side-view to enhance the sense of movement.
